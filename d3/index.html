Analysis of Age Distribution by Sex

This graph illustrates the age distribution of individuals in our dataset that have overdosed in the state of Connecticut, segmented by their sex. The 'Age' feature is represented on the x-axis, and the number of occurrences for each age group is on the y-axis. Males are shown in blue, females in pink, and any unknown or unclassified entries (marked as "Unknown" or "X") are represented in gray.
